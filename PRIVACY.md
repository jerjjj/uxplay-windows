# Privacy Policy for UxPlay Client

**Last updated: 2026-06-27**

## Data Collection

UxPlay Client does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All AirPlay streaming data (screen mirroring, audio) is processed **locally** on your device and is never sent to any external server.

## Network Usage

The app uses local network protocols (Bonjour/mDNS and AirPlay) to discover and receive streams from Apple devices on your local network. No data leaves your local network through this app.

## Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Storage

App settings (language preference, theme, AirPlay configuration) are stored locally in `%LOCALAPPDATA%\UxPlayClient\appsettings.json` and are never transmitted externally.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
